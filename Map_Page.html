Where to Find Us

Main Location

Most of the festival happens around Market Square in Burnley Town Centre. If you’re nearby, just follow the signs or music.

Getting Here

Train is easiest (Burnley Central is 8-10 mins walk). There’s parking but not loads, so maybe avoid driving if you can.

Look For

There’ll be banners, a welcome desk, and volunteers walking around with lanyards. Pretty easy to spot once you're close.

About the Festival

It’s kind of a pop-up thing, no set format. Just stalls, music, random performers. Good for wandering around.

What You’ll Find

Crafts, face painting, live stuff. Some you join in, some you watch. Bit of a mix so it doesn’t get boring.

Why It Matters

Helps local artists get noticed. Plus makes Burnley feel less empty for once. Even if art’s not your thing, it’s worth walking through.

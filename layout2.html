Arts Page

Musicians

The Burnley Arts Festival usually has live music playing during the event and it changes the whole feel of the place. It’s louder and busier, but in a good way. A lot of local musicians get to perform which is useful for people just starting out. There’s a mix of styles, some rock, some other stuff that’s harder to label. People stop, listen for a bit, then move on again.

Free Face Paint

Free face painting is also available and it’s popular with kids and families. The artists do different designs and it adds a bit more colour and fun to the festival. It gives people something to do instead of just walking around.
